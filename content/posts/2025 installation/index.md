The installation of the WM and Officers of Vermillion Lodge was a momentous occasion. Held in Prince of Wales Lodge in Minnedosa,MB along with the Other Lodges in the District.
The Lodge is proud to welcome the new officers and to celebrate the installation of the new WM Domz Gofredo Acenas. The installation ceremony was attended by many members of the Grand Lodge of Manitoba and Lodge Members of all 3 Lodges in Parkland District and their families.
